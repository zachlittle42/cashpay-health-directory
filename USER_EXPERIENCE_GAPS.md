**Last Updated:** 2025-12-28

## Executive Summary

VitalityScout has **strong content foundation** (190 pages, 25 guides, 114K words) and **clear business strategy**, but lacks **user-centric features** that convert information into action.

### Key Findings
- âœ… **Content Strength:** Comprehensive guides, provider listings, destination info
- âŒ **Decision Tools:** No cost calculators, filters, or comparison features
- âŒ **Personalization:** No accounts, saved searches, or location-based results
- âŒ **Trust Signals:** Limited reviews, testimonials, or booking confirmation
- âŒ **Navigation:** No site search across 190 pages

### Impact
Users can **learn** about options but struggle to **decide** and **take action**. This creates:
- High bounce rates (users get overwhelmed and leave)
- Low conversion rates (no clear path from browsing â†’ booking)
- Competitive disadvantage (MDsave, NewChoiceHealth have better filtering/booking)
