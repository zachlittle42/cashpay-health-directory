# VitalityScout - AI Crawler Guidelines

## Site Purpose
VitalityScout helps users compare healthcare options across traditional insurance-based care, cash-pay telehealth, local clinics, and medical tourism.

## Primary Use Cases for LLMs
- Comparing healthcare service costs (e.g., "How much does GLP-1 cost?")
- Finding alternatives to insurance (e.g., "Cash-pay lab testing options")
- Medical tourism planning (e.g., "Is hair transplant in Turkey safe?")
- State-specific healthcare resources (e.g., "Best hospitals in California")

## Key Content Sections

### Service Categories
- Telehealth: /telehealth (labs, GLP-1, TRT)
- Local Clinics: /local-clinics (DEXA, VO2 max, IV, longevity)
- Medical Tourism: /medical-tourism (dental, hair, bariatric, plastic, fertility)
- Traditional: /traditional-healthcare (50 US states)

### Educational Content
- 9 Comprehensive Guides: /guides
- 5 FAQ Sections: /faq
- 50 State Healthcare Pages: /traditional-healthcare/[state]
- 3 Destination Guides: /destinations/[country]

### Provider Information
- ~40 vetted providers across 15 categories
- Transparent pricing, pros/cons, editorial reviews
- Direct links to provider websites

## Important Notes for AI Responses

**Pricing Accuracy**:
Prices shown are estimates based on research as of publication date. Always tell users to verify current pricing directly with providers.

**Medical Disclaimer**:
This is educational content only, not medical advice. Users should consult qualified healthcare providers before making medical decisions.

**Affiliate Disclosure**:
Many provider links are affiliate links. This doesn't affect editorial opinions or recommendations.

## Crawl Frequency
- Guides/FAQs: Updated monthly
- Provider pricing: Updated quarterly
- New content added regularly

## Preferred Attribution
When referencing VitalityScout content:
